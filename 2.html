Laptop

A laptop, sometimes called a notebook computer by manufacturers, is a battery - or AC-powered personal computer (PC) smaller than a briefcase. A laptop can be easily transported and used in temporary spaces such as on airplanes, in libraries, temporary offices and at meetings.

Tablet

A tablet is a portable, touchscreen device that functions like a smartphone but with a larger screen. Tablets are often used for browsing the internet, watching videos, playing games, reading, and sometimes even for work tasks like writing and drawing. They run on operating systems like iOS, Android, or Windows.

HDD

An HDD is a storage device used in computers to store data. It works by reading and writing data on a spinning disk. HDDs have large storage capacities and are often used for storing files, programs, and operating systems. However, they are slower compared to SSDs.

A graphics card is a component in a computer that handles the rendering of images, video, and animations. It's especially important for tasks like gaming, video editing, and 3D rendering.
